## Usage
When running the Stage 2 Renderer, copy the content of *one* of these style files and paste it as context.

## Available Motifs

*   **Atlas**: Vintage cartography, warm tones, paper textures.
*   **Mythic**: Marble, classical statuary, dramatic lighting, high contrast.
*   **Systems**: Blueprint aesthetic, white lines on blue/black, technical.
*   **Sketchbook**: Graphite, ink, watercolor, rough edges, white paper.
*   **Didactic**: 1970s textbook, Helvetica, flat colors, clear diagrams.
