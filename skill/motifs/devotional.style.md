# MOTIF: DEVOTIONAL (LOCKED)

**Style Description**: Illuminated manuscripts, stained glass, sacred geometry, and religious iconography.
**Keywords**: `stained glass`, `gold leaf`, `illuminated manuscript`, `sacred geometry`, `halo`, `divine light`, `intricate patterns`, `cathedral`.

## Visual Rules
1.  **Texture**: Parchment, glass, gold foil, mosaic tiles.
2.  **Color Palette**: Jewel tones (Deep Red, Royal Blue, Emerald Green), Gold, Parchment Beige.
3.  **Lighting**: Backlit (stained glass effect), glowing halos, candlelight.
4.  **Composition**: Triptychs, circular mandalas, framed borders, hierarchical scaling.

## Design Principles (Art Direction)
*   **Typography**: Blackletter, Gothic, or Uncial.
*   **Layout**: Framed, intricate borders, heavy use of symmetry.
*   **Color Theory**: Saturated jewel tones against dark or gold backgrounds.
