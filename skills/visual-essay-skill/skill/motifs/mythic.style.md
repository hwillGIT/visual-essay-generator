# MOTIF: MYTHIC (LOCKED)

**Style Description**: Classical antiquity, stoicism, monumental architecture.
**Keywords**: `marble statue`, `classical architecture`, `dramatic lighting`, `chiaroscuro`, `cinematic`, `ethereal`, `timeless`, `stone texture`.

## Visual Rules
1.  **Texture**: Marble, stone, fog, velvet.
2.  **Color Palette**: White, grey, deep shadows (black), gold accents.
3.  **Lighting**: Dramatic side lighting, rim lighting, god rays, high contrast.
4.  **Composition**: Low angles (looking up), centered subjects, symmetry.

## Design Principles (Art Direction)
*   **Typography**: Classical Serif (Trajan, Cinzel).
*   **Layout**: Centralized, symmetrical, heavy negative space.
*   **Color Theory**: Achromatic (Black/White) with Gold Accents.
